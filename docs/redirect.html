How It Works

1
Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
2
Click any bookmark in your bookmark bar or menu.
3
The bookmark opens in a new tab while your current page stays untouched.
Note: The extension modifies your bookmark URLs with a special prefix to enable the new-tab behavior. All bookmarks are automatically restored to their original URLs when you disable or uninstall the extension.
