Privacy Policy for Open Bookmarks in New Tab

Last updated: [1st Mar]

1. DATA COLLECTION
This extension does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal 
data. All settings (enabled/disabled state, tab focus preference, 
and tab position) are stored locally on your device using 
Chrome's storage API and are never sent to any server.

2. PERMISSIONS USED
- webNavigation: Used to detect when a bookmark is clicked
- tabs: Used to open bookmarks in new tabs and restore the 
  original tab
- storage: Used to persist your settings locally

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, 
or tracking tools.

4. CHANGES
Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in extension 
updates.
